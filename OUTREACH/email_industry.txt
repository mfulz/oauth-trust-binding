OTBE addresses a long-standing but under-recognized issue in federated identity systems:
implicit trust of Authorization Servers based solely on email-domain control. This creates
silent impersonation vectors and unilateral identity assertions without explicit user consent.

The proposed extension is minimal, backward-compatible, and fully optional, but significantly
enhances privacy and reduces impersonation attack surfaces.
